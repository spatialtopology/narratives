The guards laughed at her request and Lucy suddenly realized that they must have been tipped off on when they’d be at the hideout. She turned to Max and accused him of betraying them. Max shrugged and confessed to his disloyalty, he joined the other bodyguards in line and exclaimed neither of them were leaving this hideout alive. Resigned to her fate, Lucy closed her eyes and allowed the guards to kill her and any hope the former Mayor had of retribution.
