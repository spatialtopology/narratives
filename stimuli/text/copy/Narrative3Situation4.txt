The next day the mayors successor, Mr. Smith, a man who was very unpopular due to his strong ties to the oil companies, took office. After a long day of brainstorming about the mysterious killer, Lucy decided to attend his welcome ceremony in the city. During the mayor's speech, she noticed one of the security guards looked strangely familiar. It dawned on her that this new mayor had motive to kill and that the man in black could’ve been the guard working for him.
